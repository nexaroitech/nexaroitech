Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both services for businesses and internships for students?

Yes — Nexaroitech is a software development and training company. We build websites, apps, and software for businesses, and separately run structured, mentor-led internship and training programs for students.

Are the internships paid or unpaid?

Program details, including duration and stipend eligibility, vary by track. Check the internship page or reach out on WhatsApp for the specifics of the track you're interested in.

Is the internship certificate verifiable?

Yes. Every certificate we issue can be verified on our Verify Certificate page using the certificate ID.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Yes, we work with clients remotely regardless of location. Get in touch through the contact page or WhatsApp to discuss your project.

How do I apply for an internship or training program?

Head to the Internships page and fill out the application form, or message us directly on WhatsApp using the Enquiry button.
